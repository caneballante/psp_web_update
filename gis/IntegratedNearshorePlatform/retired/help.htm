Welcome to the Puget Sound Partnerships Integrated Nearshore Platform!

You have arrived at a spatial tool that integrates a variety of assessments and data pertaining to the nearshore.

How to use:

The platform is preloaded with spatial data, categories of these data, and filters for the categories. The objective is to provide the data in a simplified format that allows the user to locate an area of interest.

The primary dataset is the PSNERP Beach Strategies for Protect, Protect High, Restore, and Restore High shorelines organized by Shoreline Process Units (SPUs) or drift cells. There are 458 SPUs in the Platform. Shorelines not included in the platform are categorized as Enhance or Enhance High in the PSNERP Beach Strategies.

Six filters are available and all values selected are applied:

1)    Verified Spawning Areas

a.    Sand Lance Spawning verified

b.    Surf Smelt Spawning verified

2)    Sediment Supply Gradient

a.    Armoring

3)    Other Habitat Services

a.    Conservation Value of Watershed Characterization, Marine Segments

4)    Risk for Future Armoring

a.    Parcel Density

b.    Land Cover Change

To simplify the evaluation, all data in the filters are categorized from 0 to 5, with 5 having the highest value for Protection or Restoration.
